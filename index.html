About Us

Thrilling Adventures: Matsu Micro Crawlers Empowerment Events

Matsu Micro Crawlers is an innovative, community-focused organization dedicated to bringing joy and adventure to children in the disability community. We specialize in organizing events that invite kids to an indoor, secure environment filled with meticulously designed mini obstacle courses. Here, they can navigate remote control rock crawlers through thrilling paths we've set up just for them.




Our Mission

Unplugged Adventures: Building Bonds Beyond Screens with Matsu Micro Crawlers"

Our core mission is to create a space where children can explore their passions and interests beyond the screen, engaging in activities that foster social interaction, physical activity, and heaps of fun. Matsu Micro Crawlers aims to offer a unique blend of excitement and camaraderie by providing activities and opportunities otherwise not available in traditional play settings.
